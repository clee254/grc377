Pups and Cups
ABOUT US

We are a local dog café located in the San Francisco Bay Area that serves specialty-roasted coffee and features adoptable dogs from San Francisco SPCA shelters once a week. Ten percent of proceeds from the coffee shop go towards supporting animal shelters in the Bay Area.

In addition to our full espresso and brew bar menu, we serve fresh made-to-order breakfast and lunch sandwiches, as well as a selection of sides and salads and other good stuff.
